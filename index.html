Light Yagami

A brilliant high school student who discovers the Death Note and attempts to cleanse the world of evil by becoming its god. His journey is one of moral ambiguity and intense psychological battles.

L Lawliet

The world’s greatest detective, tasked with discovering the identity of Kira and bringing justice. Known for his eccentric behavior and unmatched intellect, L is Light’s ultimate adversary.
